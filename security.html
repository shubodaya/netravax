Security contact guidance

Use the public enquiry form for initial contact only. Do not submit credentials, private keys, exploit payloads, customer records, regulated data or production packet captures through the website.

For authorised security work, scope, permission boundaries, test windows, target systems and reporting channels must be agreed before testing starts.

The Netravax platform remains a separate deployment from the public company website.
